MAJKIN SALAŠ

Ethno park Majkin Salash - spreads over a property of 30 ha. It is located 2.5 km from the shore of Lake Palić. The ethno park consists of two farms of Majkino and Cvetni, a pond, a small stud farm, a village yard and endless orchards. Cvetni Salaš is an accommodation facility with 15 comfortable rooms, swimming pool, meeting room, sauna, mini gym, dining room and reception. It serves for more daily rest of our guests. Mother's Farm is a restaurant of local cuisine and is located next to the Flower Farm. The old Vojvodina farm has once again become the center of life and gathering of people with a glass of good homemade brandy, original tamburitza music and traditional dishes.

Melnik

Melnik is Melnik is a town in Blagoevgrad Province, Southwestern Bulgaria, in the Southwestern Pirin Mountains, about 440 m above sea level. The town is an architectural reserve and 96 of its buildings are cultural monuments. With a population of 385, it is the smallest town in Bulgaria, retaining its town status today for historical reasons. It is situated on the foothills of the Pirin mountain range and is overlooked by the Melnik Earth Pyramids. According to archaeological evidence, the first to settle in the area were the Thracian tribe Medi to which the famous rebel Spartacus belonged.
